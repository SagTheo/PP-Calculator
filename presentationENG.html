Sport is part of my life. Doing physical activity, whatever it may be, is to me essential for anyone who aims to feel good, in their body as well as in their head. The sport we will talk about here is called calisthenics, which is bodyweight training. Calisthenics have allowed me to achieve a state of mental and physical wellness that no other activity could have, and that is precisely the reason why I wish to share my knowledge to help you gain those same benefits.

The information presented here is based on my personal experience, after years of experimentation and regular pratice. I insist on the fact that it should not be considered as exact science. It is merely a guide for those who whish to get/get back in shape.

That is the reason why the content you will have access to here was organised simply and efficiently, goal being that you can start applying it right away. A section dedicated to nutrition and another to rest were included, because even a great training plan, without the proper rest and nutrition, will not produce the desired results. You will also find other sections more training-focused.

To finish with this introduction, this site aims to help you structure your training, but also to give you the tools to improve your health as a whole. Implement incremental changes to your lifestyle using this guide, as a way to guarantee their positive effects in the long run and create new good habits.

As mentionned earlier, this is a guide, therefore it is entirely possible for you to adapt it to your own personal goals and schedule.

Happy reading and training!

One last time,

Be consistent in your efforts and persevere, results will come.

Make good choices when it comes to nutrition, prefer non-processed foods, don't starve yourself but don't overeat either.

Remember that what produces results is what you do the majority of the time.

Don't disregard rest and treat recovery, especially sleep, with care.
